Everyone loves
Free & Open Source!

Libraries, icon sets, Creative Commons, etc.

As well as
Security & Privacy

But there are challenges

  • Usability
  • Mindshare
  • Sustainability

Source of challenges

  • How many open source projects have designers on their team?
  • How many open source projects contract designers?
  • How many people work on open source full time?

So … um … what’s the problem?

Money
$$$

The funding problem

  • Traditional VCs are allergic to FOSS projects
  • Grants are time consuming & have long timelines
  • Public donations are unstable and smallish in size
  • Lots of open source projects also considered "gratis"-free

Crowdfunding is promising, but …

  • Projects often underestimate dev time & resource costs
  • Once funding runs out, can slip into crisis mode
  • “Big Launch” campaign is not ideal for existing projects
  • Good infrastructure work is usually unnoticed / unmarketable

Potential money problems

  • Can attract the wrong type of contributors
  • Reduces enthusiasm of volunteer contributors
  • It can motivate projects to make “wrong” decisions

Successful & sustainable open source projects

  • Ubuntu
  • Wikipedia
  • Mozilla
  • GNOME
  • Wordpress
  • Drupal
  • ownCloud
  • GitLab

All of these are also well-designed …

So here’s a very
rough idea

  • UI & UX design
  • Branding & marketing
  • Issue management

UI & UX design

  • Designing interfaces of the software
  • Providing mockups and reasoning
  • Improving user experience and usability
  • Conducting usability studies & user tests

Branding & marketing

  • Logo, typography, colors of public facing material
  • Designing websites of projects
  • Improving help & documentation
  • Creating videos and infographics

Issue management

  • Filtering bugs & issue tracking
  • Creating new issues from usability studies
  • Sprint & milestone planning
  • Working with developers of projects
